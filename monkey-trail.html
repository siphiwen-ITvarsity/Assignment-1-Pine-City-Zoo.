MONKEY TRAIL

Monkey Trail is a 16.1 kilometer moderately trafficked loop trail that features beautiful wild flowers and is rated as moderate. The trail is primarily used for hiking, running, horses, and mountain biking and is accessible year-round. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is to the north-east of the main entrance.
